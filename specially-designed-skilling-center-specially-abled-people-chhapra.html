Specially designed skilling center for specially- abled people in Chhapra

Employment is often seen as an important step towards the rehabilitation and empowerment of a specially-abled person, because it provides a sense of belonging, importance and independence. Being able to work and earn a sustainable amount of income is an effective way of evading social exclusion and poverty. However, specially-abled people find it very difficult to be accepted as an active and functional member of the society. The chief obstacles in achieving employment are societal bias, individual circumstances (poverty, lack of skills), and physical limitations (bad health, lack of transport and accessibility).

Safeducate, in association with Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, launched a container school in Chhapra, Bihar to facilitate the skill development initiatives for physically disabled young students. We have got affiliated with Skill Council for Persons with Disabilities (SCPwD) to launch short-term courses for these under privileged students. Safeducate will train the students in Retail Sales Associate job role under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) project.

The initiative was inagurated by Hon. Minister of Social Justice and Empowerment Shri. Thawar Chand Gehlot and Hon. Minister of State for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Shri. Rajiv Pratap Rudy.
