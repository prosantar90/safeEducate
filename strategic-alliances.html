Strategic Alliances

Home/ About us / Strategic Alliances

Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship MSDE

A trusted member of the Skill Ministry since inception, Safeducate has always proactively partnered with all initiatives & activities of MSDE

National Skill Development Corporation NSDC

Safeducate became the first training partner of NSDC in the Logistics sector in 2013

Ministry of Rural Development MoRD

Safeducate is the Champion Employer organization for MoRD and has been running its flagship skill development project Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) in multiple states in India

All India Management Association AIMA

Strategic and Knowledge partnership to integrate SCM & Logistics with Management education

Chartered Institute of Logistics and Transportation CILT

Technical and knowledge partnership to design, develop and run SCM & Logistics courses covering global standards and practices

Logistics Sector Skill Council (LSC)

Safeducate is the first & the largest training partner of LSC; Safeducate’s Founder & CEO Ms.Divya Jain is also the member of LSC’s Governing Council

Automotive Skill Development Council ASDC

Safeducate is affiliated with ASDC to train people in various job roles in the Automotive sector

Retailers Associations Skill Council of India RASCI

Safeducate is affiliated with RASCI to train people in various job roles in the Retail sector

The National Association of Software and Services Companies NASSCOM

Safeducate is affiliated with NASSCOM to train people in various job roles in the IT&ITeS sector

Skill Council for Persons with Disabilities SCPwD

Safeducate is associated with SCPwD to train specially-abled students through various specifically designed programs
